Annotated reading list

Foundational and recent references for every topic in the roadmap. Listed by area, with one-line annotations and a BibTeX block at the bottom.

1 · Molecular spin qubits

  • Atzori & Sessoli, JACS 141 (2019) — "The second quantum revolution: role and challenges of molecular chemistry." The canonical perspective paper.
  • Atzori et al., JACS 138 (2016) — Room-temperature quantum coherence in [VO(dmit)2]2−; demonstrates $T_2 \sim$ µs.
  • Bader et al., Nat. Commun. 5 (2014) — Room-temperature coherence in [V(C8S8)3]2−.
  • Yamabayashi et al., JACS 140 (2018) — Vanadyl phthalocyanine VOPc: $T_2 \approx 1\,\mu$s at 80 K with isotopic engineering.
  • Zadrozny et al., ACS Cent. Sci. 1 (2015) — Millisecond coherence in nuclear-spin-free vanadyl complex.
  • Ishikawa et al., JACS 125 (2003) — Original TbPc2 single-molecule magnet.
  • Thiele et al., Science 344 (2014) — Electrical detection of TbPc2 nuclear-spin qubit.
  • Shiddiq et al., Nature 531 (2016) — Clock transitions for protected molecular qubits.
  • Goodwin et al., Nature 548 (2017) — Dy-SIM with blocking temperature above 60 K.
  • Coronado, Nat. Rev. Mater. 5 (2020) — Molecular magnetism for quantum technologies (review).
  • Wernsdorfer & Ruben, Adv. Mater. 31 (2019) — Synthetic hilbert spaces in molecular magnets (review).

2 · Hybrid spin–cavity platforms

  • Mergenthaler et al., PRL 119 (2017) — Strong coupling of microwave photons to molecular spin ensemble.
  • Ghirri et al., Magnetochemistry 3 (2017) — Coherent coupling between molecular spins and a SC resonator.
  • Bonizzoni et al., npj Quantum Inf. 6 (2020) — Storage and retrieval in hybrid SC-molecular memory.
  • Gimeno et al., ACS Nano 14 (2020) — Lanthanide molecular qubit coupled to a SC resonator.
  • Carretta et al., Magnetochemistry 7 (2021) — A perspective on molecular spin processors.
  • Chiesa et al., JPCL 11 (2020) — Molecular nanomagnets as qubits with tunable coupling.

3 · Superconducting qubits & cQED

  • Krantz et al., Appl. Phys. Rev. 6 (2019) — "A quantum engineer's guide to superconducting qubits." Required reading.
  • Koch et al., PRA 76 (2007) — Original transmon proposal.
  • Manucharyan et al., Science 326 (2009) — Fluxonium.
  • Blais et al., RMP 93 (2021) — Circuit QED review.
  • Wallraff et al., Nature 431 (2004) — Original demonstration of strong coupling in cQED.
  • Reagor et al., PRB 94 (2016) — Quantum memory with millisecond coherence in a 3D cavity.
  • Heeres et al., Nat. Commun. 8 (2017) — Cavity quantum control with optimal pulses.

4 · Schrieffer–Wolff & effective Hamiltonians

  • Bravyi, DiVincenzo & Loss, Ann. Phys. 326 (2011) — Modern Schrieffer–Wolff transformation.
  • Cohen-Tannoudji, Diu & Laloë, Quantum Mechanics, Vol. 2 — Reference for SW and effective Hamiltonians.
  • Blanes et al., Phys. Rep. 470 (2009) — Magnus expansion in time-dependent perturbation theory.
  • Magesan & Gambetta, PRA 101 (2020) — Effective Hamiltonian models of the cross-resonance gate.
  • Goldman & Dalibard, PRX 4 (2014) — Floquet engineering for ultracold systems.

5 · Tree tensor networks & open-system TDVP

  • Bridgeman & Chubb, J. Phys. A 50 (2017) — "Hand-waving and interpretive dance" tensor-network primer.
  • Haegeman et al., PRB 94 (2016) — TDVP for matrix product states.
  • Bauernfeind & Aichhorn, SciPost Phys. 8 (2020) — Tree tensor networks for impurity models.
  • Schröder, Strathearn & Lovett, Nat. Commun. 10 (2019) — Tensor-network methods for non-Markovian open systems.
  • Cui, Cirac & Bañuls, PRL 114 (2015) — Variational MPS for Lindbladians.
  • Werner et al., PRL 116 (2016) — Positive tensor-network for Lindblad master equations.

6 · Quantum error correction

  • Bravyi & Kitaev, quant-ph/9811052 (1998) — Surface code on planar topology.
  • Dennis, Kitaev, Landahl & Preskill, J. Math. Phys. 43 (2002) — "Topological quantum memory." Threshold analysis of the toric/surface code.
  • Fowler et al., PRA 86 (2012) — "Surface codes: towards practical large-scale quantum computation." The engineering reference.
  • Bombin, PRL 105 (2010) — Original color code.
  • Landahl, Anderson & Rice, arXiv:1108.5738 (2011) — Color-code threshold estimates.
  • Acharya et al., Nature 614 (2023) — Below-threshold surface code on Google Sycamore.
  • Krinner et al., Nature 605 (2022) — Distance-3 surface code in transmons.
  • Higgott, ACM Trans. Quantum Comput. 3 (2022) — PyMatching: minimum-weight perfect-matching decoder.

7 · Software tools

  • QuTiP — Reference Lindblad solver for validation.
  • Stim — Stabilizer simulator used for the M8 decoder benchmark.
  • PyMatching — MWPM decoder.
  • PyTorch — LindbladTTN tensor backend.
  • torchdiffeq — Adaptive ODE integrator (VMF strategy).
  • pytenso — Sister project whose design LindbladTTN follows.
